## Full description

KitaMo helps Filipino small sellers track sales, inventory, grocery costs, recipes, fixed costs, and profit across stalls — locally on one Android device.

Para sa sari-sari store, karinderia, street food, at night-market sellers:

- **Kiosk selling** — fast checkout with cash, GCash, Maya, or bank reference, bundle prices (like 8 for ₱150), and text receipts you can copy or share.
- **Paninda at stock** — products, stock counts, low-stock alerts, at Notify Owner from the selling counter.
- **Grocery Pool** — record grocery purchases per brand and source (Kikkoman vs local soy sauce) with automatic cost per unit.
- **Recipes** — cost each recipe from the exact grocery items you picked; see cost per piece, how many you can still make, and which ingredient runs out first.
- **Production (Niluto)** — produce for a stall; ingredients deduct automatically and the cost follows the goods.
- **Cook-upon-order** — made-to-order items compute their cost at sale time and never block a sale even when grocery stock is low.
- **Transfers, Nasayang, at Fixed Costs** — move goods between stalls, log spoilage with its value, and track rent, sweldo, kuryente, at iba pang bayarin with due-date reminders in-app.
- **Profit Reports** — per-stall and whole-business: revenue, puhunan, fixed costs, sayang, at net profit — today, this week, this month, or all.

**Data stays on your phone.** Walang account, walang cloud, walang ads. Everything is saved locally on this device only.

**Honest pilot notes** — wala pa sa version na ito:
- Cloud backup or sync (kapag nawala ang phone o na-uninstall ang app, mawawala ang data)
- Login/accounts
- AI assistant (the Helper answers from your phone's records only)
- Camera/receipt scanning
- Bluetooth printing
- BIR/official-receipt or tax compliance features
- Payment processing (references are recorded manually; the app does not move money)
